What is a First-Generation Student?

A first-generation college student is someone whose parent(s) or guardian(s) did not earn a four-year bachelor's degree. First-generation students often bring unique perspectives and experiences to campus, and may face distinct challenges as they navigate their college journey. The First-Generation Student Success program is dedicated to providing comprehensive support, resources, and community to help our first-generation students thrive academically, socially, and professionally.
